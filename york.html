About Me

My name is Jaime Dantas, and I am a Computer Engineer from Brazil. This web page contains information about all my professional and academic experiences, and it was developed as a reference page for my master’s degree application in Computer Science at York University. Currently, I work as a Software Engineer in the biggest bank of Latin America where I design, implement, and maintain systems in a highly available, real-time distributed platform. Not only do I use public cloud providers, but also maintain private cloud platforms. In addition to my career in the finance area, I worked in several engineering fields in a wide range of industries such as the aerospace, energy and telecommunication ones. Please, consider my application for the master's program in Computer Science at York University.
